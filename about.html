About Us

Mission Statement

We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality and most advanced products in the water filtration industry. By using advanced materials and cutting edge concepts, we are changing the world with clean water—one person at a time.

Company Overview

The McIlveen family started an injection molding company that manufactures plastic parts in 2005 called Celtic Plastics LLC. Clear Diamond Water, a subsidiary of Celtic Plastics, manufactures water filter housings for the recreational vehicle industry, water heater industry, and reverse osmosis systems industry. The family-owned business, in partnership with Eastman Chemical, has developed a groundbreaking product line of clear plastic products using Eastman’s Eastar copolyester resin. Clear Diamond Water is revolutionizing the water filtration housing industry with its patent pending process and product.
