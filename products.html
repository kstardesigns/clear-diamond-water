Polaris Twin P/N: CDW101

  • Product Description: two clear filter housings, two NPT threaded lids (½ inch or ¾ inch), two brackets, one wrench, one connecter, one ceramic filter, and one carbon block filter (NSF Tested)
  • Twin Filter In-House System
  • The ceramic filter removes all bacteria when added to water sources that do not contain chlorine. It removes 99.9999% of bacteria and removes suspended solids, pathogenic bacteria and cysts. The self-sterilizing filter is silver impregnated to prevent bacteria growth.
  • The carbon block filter removes lead, silt, heavy metals, and gives water a clean taste.
  • Clear Diamond Water only uses a clear filter housing so that the consumer can visually see their water quality. The material used is a space age material that meets and exceeds all NSF tests and withstands freezing temperatures.(NSF Tested)
  • Ideal uses for the Polaris Twin:
    • Campers/Recreational vehicles
    • Homes
    • Remote cabins with a power source
    • Preppers, survivalists, or groups that live off the grid but have a source of power
    • Third world countries
